How to export

  1. Select items to sell in EVE
  2. Right-click -> Sell Items (n)
  3. Click Export in the sell window
  4. Paste the result here
  5. Select your station above
  6. Click Fetch Prices
  7. Copy output -> Import Prices... in EVE
